Digitalization: Trend Meets Technology
The footwear industry is a wide range of technology from traditional and advanced techniques like 3D printing and smart materials. Artificial intelligence-based shoes are footwear equipped with AI technology that provide personalized comfort and performance enhancing features, such as automatic adjustments to fit and gait analysis. Due to the use of various materials, the variety of product types and shoes making remains largely manual. The latest advances and the emergence of new technologies. The quick advancement and widespread use of technology has fundamentally changed how people work, live, communicate and engage with one another. Although technology is helping humans in many positive ways, as we know everything has both merits and demerits. Just like that, technology also has both positive aspects and negative aspects.

Technology is enhancing day by day with many more features and it has many positive impacts in human life. It has permanently altered our everyday lives, the way we used to live, work, interact and communicate with others, it has transformed several fields like communication, education, healthcare and general advancement. Along with communication, the transformation in education is also one of the most significant impacts of technology. People can get various shoes collection such as Sneakers, oxford, running shoes, climbing shoes etc. It can also be available online shopping and in the supermarket. Technology has become a key factor in advancement, affecting everything from shop and online based and how people communicate and buy the products.

Although we cannot deny that technology has brought many positive changes, it is necessary to acknowledge that along with the positive impact its rapid growth has also had some negative impact on human life. As we all know, technology has brought too many social platforms through which humans can digitally communicate with each other and due to this there less interaction between people. People might communicate virtually more often than communicating personally which may result in feelings of loneliness and decrease in the quality of interactions between people. Also, the footwear industry currently faces some challenges due to ever changing consumer preferences, manage the cost of goods or raw materials, human resources and financial challenges in the market strategies.

The use of technology in the footwear industry is to provide personalized comfort and enhance enhanced features, such as automatic adjustments to fitness and gait analysis. And protection against adversities of the environment such as wear from rough ground, stability on slippery ground, and temperature.

In conclusion, we can say that technology has a dynamic impact on human life in every sector. It is constantly evolving human life and creating a foundation for future which will be even more inventive and creative. But, along with the advancement of technology it may bring some drawbacks, so it is necessary to recognize and deal with it and also we need to be prepared for upcoming one.
